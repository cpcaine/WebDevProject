Yoga

A mind-body connection

As I have moved to college, I have been able to spend more time prioritizing my health and well-being.

Yoga and pilates classes are some of the best ways I have strengthened my mentality and physicality.

Yoga is a mind-body practice that offers numerous benefits in all aspects of personal wellness.

Here are some of the physical benefits:

  1. Healthy flexibility
    • Regular yoga practice stretches muscles
    • Increases the range of motion in joints
    • Reduce stiffness and improve posture
  2. Enhanced strength
    • Yoga builds strength in all muscle groups but particularly the core, arms, and legs
  3. Posture
    • Yoga promotes body awareness, leading to better alignment
    • Reduces risks of back and neck injuries or pain
  4. Balance and Coordination
    • Balancing poses in yoga practices improve stability and coordination
  5. Connection with breath
    • Yoga classes are full of breathing exercises which enhance lung capacity

Importantly, here are some of the mental benefits:

  1. Reduces stress
    • Yoga promotes a calm nervous system and reduces cortisol (stress) levels
    • Focus on the breath in meditation allow for healthy relaxation
  2. Improved concentration
    • Allows for the mind to stay present and focused in class and in other activities
    • Many of the regular yoga practice movements require concentration which improve cognitive function
  3. Emotional regulation
    • Yoga creates a time for emotion awareness and helps with responding to stress
    • Can help reduce strong emotions such as stress, frustration, and anxiety
  4. Improved sleep quality due to relaxtion techniques taught in the practice
  5. Boosts confidence!
  6. Enhanced mind-body connecion
